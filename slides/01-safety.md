# ⛑️ Requirement 1: Safety

## Staying Healthy While You Code

---

# 📋 What This Requirement Asks

**(a)** Watch the **"Digital Safety"** video from Scouting America's Personal Safety Awareness series (with parent/guardian permission).

**(b)** Discuss **first aid and prevention** for injuries that can happen during programming:
- Repetitive stress injuries (RSI)
- Eyestrain

---

# 🔒 Part A: Digital Safety

Before we talk about physical safety, let's talk about **digital safety**.

### Key Points:
- 🔑 Use **strong, unique passwords** for every account
- 🙈 **Never share** personal info (address, school, phone #) online
- 🤔 **Think before you click** — phishing emails look real!
- 📸 What you post online **stays online forever**
- 🗣️ **Tell a trusted adult** if something feels wrong

> 📺 Watch: Scouting America "Digital Safety" video (ask your counselor for the link)

---

# 🏥 Part B: What is RSI?

**RSI = Repetitive Stress Injury**

This happens when you do the same motion over and over — like typing or using a mouse for hours.

### Common RSI Types for Programmers:
| Injury | What It Is | Where It Hurts |
|--------|-----------|----------------|
| Carpal Tunnel Syndrome | Nerve gets squeezed in your wrist | Wrist, hand, fingers |
| Tendinitis | Tendons get inflamed | Wrist, elbow, shoulder |
| Trigger Finger | Finger gets stuck bent | Fingers |

---

# ⚠️ RSI Warning Signs

Watch out for these symptoms after coding sessions:

- 😣 **Pain or aching** in wrists, hands, arms, or shoulders
- 🥶 **Numbness or tingling** in fingers
- 💪 **Weakness** — dropping things
- 🔴 **Swelling** in hands or wrists
- 🦾 **Stiffness** — hard to move your fingers

### If you feel these:
1. **STOP** what you're doing
2. **Rest** the affected area
3. **Tell an adult** if it doesn't get better
4. **Ice** the area if swollen (20 min on, 20 min off)

---

# 🛡️ Preventing RSI — Take Breaks!

### The **20-20-20 Break Rule** (adapted for movement!)

Every **20 minutes**:
- ✋ Take a **20-second break** from typing
- 🤸 **Stretch** your hands and wrists
- 🚶 Stand up and **move around** every hour

---

# 🛡️ Preventing RSI — Good Habits

### Daily Coding Habits:
- Keep your wrists **straight** (not bent up or down)
- Don't **pound** the keys — type gently
- Use **keyboard shortcuts** to reduce mouse use
- Switch hands with the mouse sometimes

---

# 🖥️ Ergonomic Setup

### Your Ideal Coding Station:

```
        👀 Eyes level with top of screen
         |
    ┌─────────┐
    │ Monitor  │  ← arm's length away (20-26 inches)
    └─────────┘
   ─────────────   ← desk at elbow height
  ⌨️  🖱️           ← keyboard & mouse at same level
         |
    ┌─────────┐
    │  Chair   │  ← feet flat on floor
    └─────────┘      back supported
        🦶🦶
```

- **Monitor:** top of screen at eye level
- **Chair:** feet flat on floor, back straight
- **Arms:** elbows at ~90° angle

---

# 👁️ What is Eyestrain?

Staring at screens for a long time can cause:

- 😫 **Tired, sore eyes**
- 🌫️ **Blurry vision**
- 🤕 **Headaches**
- 👁️ **Dry eyes** (you blink less when staring at screens!)
- 😰 **Trouble focusing** on things far away

### Fun Fact:
The average person blinks **15-20 times per minute** normally, but only **3-4 times** when staring at a screen! 😮

---

<!-- _class: compact -->

# 👁️ The 20-20-20 Rule for Eyes

Every **20 minutes**, look at something **20 feet away** for **20 seconds**.

### More Eye-Friendly Tips:

| Tip | Why |
|-----|-----|
| 💡 Match screen brightness to room | Reduces strain |
| 📏 Sit arm's length from your screen | Not too close! |
| 🌙 Use "dark mode" or "night shift" | Less blue light |
| 💧 Keep eye drops nearby | For dry eyes |
| 🔤 Increase font size in your editor | Easier to read code |
| 🪟 Code near a window if possible | Natural light helps |

---

# 🏋️ Let's Stretch! — Hands & Wrists

### Hand & Wrist Stretches (Do This Together!):

1. **Prayer stretch** 🙏 — Press palms together, hold 15 sec
2. **Fist clench** ✊ — Make a fist, then spread fingers wide (×10)
3. **Wrist circles** 🔄 — Rotate wrists slowly in circles (×10 each way)
4. **Finger spread** 🖐️ — Spread fingers as wide as you can, hold 5 sec (×5)

---

# 🏋️ Let's Stretch! — Neck & Shoulders

### Neck & Shoulder Stretches (Do This Together!):

5. **Shoulder rolls** — Forward ×10, backward ×10
6. **Neck tilts** — Ear to shoulder, hold 10 sec each side
7. **Look up!** — Tilt head back gently, hold 10 sec

> **Do these stretches every time you take a coding break!**

---

# 🩹 First Aid Review — R.I.C.E.

If someone has an RSI flare-up or injury while coding:

### **R.I.C.E.** Method:
| Letter | Action | What to Do |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **R** | Rest | Stop the activity immediately |
| **I** | Ice | Apply ice pack for 20 min (wrap in cloth!) |
| **C** | Compress | Light wrap with bandage if swollen |
| **E** | Elevate | Raise the hurt area above heart level |

---

# 🩹 First Aid Review — When to Get Help

### See a doctor if:
- Pain lasts more than a few days
- Numbness or tingling won't go away
- You can't grip things normally
